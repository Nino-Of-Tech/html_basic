This is the 3rd Ikaze event that will be hosted by the ALU Community of students for the 2022 Intake of new students. The event will be held on the 23rd of September, 2022 by 3:00pm. Join us for a blast event. You don't want to miss it!

The Walk of Nations

The walk of nations would showcase new students from different parts of Africa. Students from all across Africa would be seen wearing their cultural and native attires. Alongside showcasing their cultures, students will also have the chance to learn and understand other cultures from around Africa ranging from Zulu, Igbo, Hausa, Fafra, Shona, and so on. You are welcome to come and see the lovely cultures in Africa
